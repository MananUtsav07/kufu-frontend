Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need technical skills to set it up? expand_more
No technical skills are required. Our team handles the initial setup, and the dashboard is designed to be as simple as editing a text document.
Does the AI sound human? expand_more
Yes! We use advanced LLMs (Large Language Models) tailored with your specific brand voice to ensure conversations feel natural and helpful.
Can it handle my specific industry knowledge? expand_more
Absolutely. During onboarding, you provide us with your brochures, price lists, and FAQs, which we use to train your custom AI brain.
Which languages does the AI support? expand_more
Our AI supports over 50 languages, including English, Spanish, French, German, Portuguese, and Mandarin.
Can it sync with my CRM? expand_more
Yes, we integrate with popular CRMs like HubSpot, Salesforce, Pipedrive, and through Zapier for custom workflows.
What happens if the AI gets stuck? expand_more
You can set up a "human handoff" where the AI notifies you or your team instantly via Slack, Email, or WhatsApp if a customer requests a human.
